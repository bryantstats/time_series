| 2 | 3     |
| 3 | 5     |
| 4 | 8     |
| 5 | 12    |
| 6 | 13    |
| 7 | 16    |

  a. Calculate the moving average series with  $k = 2$
  
  b. Calculate the moving average series with  $k = 3$

  c. Calculate the double moving average series with  $k = 3$
